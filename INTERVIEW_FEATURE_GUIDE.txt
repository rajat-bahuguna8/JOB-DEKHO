═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  FOR COMPANIES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

HOW TO SCHEDULE AN INTERVIEW:

  Step 1: Login as company (e.g., hr@techcorp.com / company123)
  
  Step 2: Go to "My Jobs"
  
  Step 3: Click "View Applications" on any job
  
  Step 4: Find a candidate you want to interview
  
  Step 5: In the status dropdown, select "Interview Scheduled"
  
  Step 6: Click "Update" button
  
  ✅ Done! The student will now see "Interview" status
